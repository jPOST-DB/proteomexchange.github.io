Mission

The ProteomeXchange consortium has been set up to provide a globally coordinated submission of mass spectrometry proteomics data to the main existing proteomics repositories, and to encourage optimal data dissemination. See the Nature Biotechnology paper published in 2014.

Public Data

Public PXD datasets can be browsed over at ProteomeCentral.

Member repositories and data submission

The founding members of ProteomeXchange are PRIDE, the PRoteomics IDEntifications Database at the European Bioinformatics Institute, based in Hinxton, UK and PeptideAtlas, part of the Institute of Systems Biology in Seattle, USA.
